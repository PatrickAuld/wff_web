WearOS Watch Face Format v4 — official samples rendered to HTML Canvas

android/wear-os-samples/WatchFaceFormat

SimpleAnalog

Analog clock with colored theme configuration, background annulus, shadow hands, and ambient mode. Hand images are device assets.

SimpleDigital

Digital clock with SYNC_TO_DEVICE font, ambient mode weight variant, and localized greeting string.

Flavors

Digital clock with three user-selectable Flavors combining color theme, clock appearance, and heart rate display.

Weather

Digital clock with live weather data: current conditions, hourly forecast icons, and daily forecast. Falls back gracefully when weather is unavailable.

PhotosMask

Digital clock with user photo displayed through a rounded hexagon mask (SINGLE photo configuration).

PhotosMulti

Digital clock with cycling user photo gallery (MULTIPLE photo configuration, changes on tap or after every 3 views).
